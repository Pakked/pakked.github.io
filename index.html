THE GAME

Ares Royale is a Micro-Royale created using Unreal Editor For Fortnite (UEFN), The game features new locations to explore in an ever-expanding world. And a Unique Loot Pool of Timeless Weapons. Divisions, The Ares Store and more new mechanics to use to your advantage.



Ares Royale is currently in development and will be released as soon as its ready.

DIVISIONS

Divisions are a new way to change the game. Choose your divsion at the beginning of the game, Divisions are unique ability and item presets that give you an advantage against other players.

[Abilities and Items are specific to Divisons]

ARES STORE

The Ares Store allows you to buy better loot. The Store can ping on the map at any point during a game.

[Reach the store before other players for better loot]

INSTANCES

Find Instances and recieve a random effect. (eg. Health + Shield Regeneration, Give Random Weapon, Spawn Rift, Give Player Speed etc.)
